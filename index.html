Help & Usage Guide

Control Descriptions

  • Search Keywords:

    Enter terms and use Boolean operators (AND, OR, NOT).

  • Price Range ($):

    Minimum and maximum price (supports cents).

  • % Off Discount ≥:

    Filter by discount percentage (5% steps from 5% up to 100%).

  • Customer Review:

    Filter by star rating (1★ through 5★).

  • Sort By:

    Order results by relevance, price low→high, price high→low, review, or newest.

  • Include Brands:

    Tag brands to include; check “Match only these brands” to exclude all others.

  • Exclude Brands:

    Tag brands to exclude.

  • Prime Only:

    Show only Prime-eligible products.

  • In Stock:

    Show only currently in-stock items.

  • Free Shipping:

    Filter for free-shipping items.

  • Sold & Shipped by Amazon:

    Only Amazon-sold items.

  • Lightning Deals:

    Active lightning deals only.

  • Coupons Available:

    Items with coupons.

  • Subscribe & Save:

    Subscribe & Save eligible items.

  • Amazon’s Choice:

    Amazon’s Choice products.

  • Best Seller:

    Amazon Best Sellers.

  • Free Returns:

    Items with free returns option.

  • Gift Wrap Available:

    Items offering gift wrap.

  • Enable Boolean Operators:

    Turn on/off Boolean parsing in keywords.

  • Field Filters:

    Advanced field queries (e.g. field-brand:Sony).

  • ASIN/ISBN Direct Lookup:

    Jump to a product by ASIN, ISBN or UPC.

  • Price Drop ≥ X% Below 90-Day Avg:

    Filter by drop vs. 90-day average price.

  • Coupon-Clippable Only:

    Show only coupon-clippable items.

  • Warehouse / Open-Box:

    Warehouse and open-box deals.

  • ▼ % vs. Avg (30/60/90-Day):

    Filter by price drop vs. short-term average.

  • ▲ Max 7-Day Volatility:

    Limit by 7-day price volatility.

  • Side-by-Side Spec Tables:

    Compare specs of two products side by side.

  • Verified-Purchase Only Reviews:

    Show only reviews with verified purchase.

  • Buy-Now-or-Wait Score:

    AI-driven purchase timing recommendation.

  • Restock Forecasts:

    Enter ASIN to see restock estimates.

  • Emotion-Feedback Loop:

    AI-driven emotional insights on purchases.
  • (Coming Soon): URL Parameter Builder, API Scratchpad, Visual & Voice Interfaces, Niche & Lifestyle Filters, Social & Collaborative features.
