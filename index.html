Help & Usage Guide

  • Search Keywords: Enter terms and use Boolean operators (AND, OR, NOT).
  • Price Range ($): Minimum and maximum price (supports cents).
  • % Off Discount ≥: Filter by discount percentage (5% steps up to 100%).
  • Customer Review: Filter by star rating (1★ to 5★).
  • Sort By: Order by relevance, price, review or arrival date.
  • Include/Exclude Brands: Tagify inputs to include or exclude brands.
  • Prime Only: Show only Prime-eligible items.
  • In Stock: Show only currently in-stock items.
  • Free Shipping: Filter free-shipping items.
  • Sold & Shipped by Amazon: Only Amazon-sold.
  • Lightning Deals: Active lightning deals only.
  • Coupons Available: Items with coupons.
  • Subscribe & Save: Subscribe-eligible items.
  • Amazon’s Choice: Amazon’s Choice products.
  • Best Seller: Amazon best sellers.
  • Free Returns: Items with free returns.
  • Gift Wrap Available: Gift-wrap options.
  • Enable Boolean Operators: Enable/disable Boolean in keywords.
  • Field Filters: Advanced field queries.
  • ASIN/ISBN Direct Lookup: Direct product lookup.
  • Price Drop vs Avg: Percent drop vs 30/60/90-day avg.
  • Max 7-Day Volatility: Filter by volatility.
  • Comparison & Research Aids: Side-by-side specs, verified reviews.
  • Personalization & AI-Driven: Predictive scores, restock forecasts, emotion feedback.
  • (Coming Soon): URL builder, API scratchpad, visual/voice UI, niche filters, social features.
