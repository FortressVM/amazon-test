Amazon Super Search

Amazon Super Search is a powerful, web-based tool designed for savvy shoppers who want to uncover in-stock lightning deals, set deep-discount thresholds, and track price drops across Amazon’s vast catalog. Tailored for busy bargain hunters, it filters products by free shipping, Prime eligibility, and customer ratings—so you can zero in on the best offers in seconds. Never waste time sifting through endless listings again: find exactly what you want at the price you want, every time.

How-To Guide
Using the Price Filter
  1. Type your Min & Max amounts.
  2. Click “Search Amazon” to apply.
  3. Clear values to reset.
Brand Filters
  1. Start typing a brand name.
  2. Check “Match only…” to exclude all others.
  3. Uncheck to broaden your search.
Deal-Hunter Goodies
  1. Set your minimum discount
    In the “Price Drop” box, type the smallest percentage off you’re interested in.
    Example: entering 20 will only show items that are at least 20% below their normal price.
  2. Filter by coupons or open-box
    Tick Coupon-Clippable Only if you want only items that have an Amazon coupon you can clip at checkout.
    Tick Warehouse / Open-Box if you’d rather see only warehouse-sold or refurbished deals.
  3. Fine-tune using recent averages
    Below that you’ll see fields like “▼ % vs. 30-Day Avg” (and 60-, 90-day). Use these to find products whose current price is that much lower than its average over the last month (or two, or three).
    And if you want to avoid wildly swinging prices, set the “▲ Max 7-Day Volatility” number to cap how much a product’s price is allowed to change day-to-day.

Taken together, this section helps you zero in on deep, stable bargains—only showing you items that hit your discount threshold, come with coupons or open-box pricing, and aren’t bouncing all over the place.

Quick Tips: How to Find the Best Deals
  1. Start with a broad keyword search (e.g. “wireless earbuds”).
  2. Use the “% Off” filter for deep discounts.
  3. Tick “Lightning Deals” & “Coupons Available.”
  4. Sort by “Best Sellers” or “Avg. Customer Review.”
  5. Combine with “In Stock” + “Prime Only.”
Using Boolean Operators
  1. ✔ Check “Enable Boolean Operators”.
  2. ✔ In “Search Keywords” use AND/OR or prefix NOT.
  3. ✔ Click “Search Amazon” to run the query.
How to Find the Best Deals
  1. Use the Deal-Hunter Goodies section.
  2. Enter a % drop (e.g. “20”) in Price Drop.
  3. Tick “Coupon-Clippable Only” or “Warehouse / Open-Box.”
  4. Set ▼ % vs. 30/60/90-day averages.
  5. Use ▲ Max 7-Day Volatility to limit swings.
  6. Combine with Lightning Deals & Prime Only.
Applying Field Filters
  1. ✔ In “Field Filters”, type field-brand:Sony.
  2. ✔ Separate rules with commas (e.g. field-price:100-200,field-rating:4).
  3. ✔ Click “Search Amazon” to see exact matches.
Doing an ASIN / ISBN Lookup
  1. ✔ Paste an ASIN/ISBN/UPC into “Direct Lookup”.
  2. ✔ Click “Search Amazon.”
  3. ✔ The exact product opens in a new tab.
Build URL Parameters Manually
  1. Fill in keywords → k=…, filters → rh=….
  2. Copy the generated URL from the “Copy Link” box.
  3. Bookmark or share that URL to reapply the exact filters.
Filter by Seller Type
  1. Tick “Sold & Shipped by Amazon.”
  2. Tick “Warehouse / Open-Box.”
  3. Tick “Coupons Available” for extra savings.
  4. Click “Search Amazon” to see only your chosen sources.
Use Field-Level Filters
  1. Open “Power-User Tools.”
  2. Enter rules like field-brand:Sony,field-price:50-100.
  3. Click “Search Amazon” for hyper-targeted results.
Sort Top-Rated Bargains
  1. Select your %-Off threshold.
  2. Choose “Avg. Customer Review.”
  3. Sort by “Best Sellers.”
  4. Click “Search Amazon” to surface top deals.
Share Custom Searches
  1. Apply all your filters and click “Search Amazon.”
  2. Copy the URL from the “Copy Link” box.
  3. Paste it or use the share buttons to send to friends.

Help & Usage Guide

Control Descriptions

  • Search Keywords:

    Enter terms and use Boolean operators (AND, OR, NOT).

  • Price Range ($):

    Minimum and maximum price (supports cents).

  • % Off Discount ≥:

    Filter by discount percentage (5% steps from 5% up to 100%).

  • Customer Review:

    Filter by star rating (1★ through 5★).

  • Sort By:

    Order results by relevance, price low→high, price high→low, review, or newest.

  • Include Brands:

    Tag brands to include; check “Match only these brands” to exclude all others.

  • Exclude Brands:

    Tag brands to exclude.

  • Prime Only:

    Show only Prime-eligible products.

  • In Stock:

    Show only currently in-stock items.

  • Free Shipping:

    Filter for free-shipping items.

  • Sold & Shipped by Amazon:

    Only Amazon-sold items.

  • Lightning Deals:

    Active lightning deals only.

  • Coupons Available:

    Items with coupons.

  • Subscribe & Save:

    Subscribe & Save eligible items.

  • Amazon’s Choice:

    Amazon’s Choice products.

  • Best Seller:

    Amazon Best Sellers.

  • Free Returns:

    Items with free returns option.

  • Gift Wrap Available:

    Items offering gift wrap.

  • Enable Boolean Operators:

    Turn on/off Boolean parsing in keywords.

  • Field Filters:

    Advanced field queries (e.g. field-brand:Sony).

  • ASIN/ISBN Direct Lookup:

    Jump to a product by ASIN, ISBN or UPC.

  • Price Drop ≥ X% Below 90-Day Avg:

    Filter by drop vs. 90-day average price.

  • Coupon-Clippable Only:

    Show only coupon-clippable items.

  • Warehouse / Open-Box:

    Warehouse and open-box deals.

  • ▼ % vs. Avg (30/60/90-Day):

    Filter by price drop vs. short-term average.

  • ▲ Max 7-Day Volatility:

    Limit by 7-day price volatility.

  • Side-by-Side Spec Tables:

    Compare specs of two products side by side.

  • Verified-Purchase Only Reviews:

    Show only reviews with verified purchase.

  • Buy-Now-or-Wait Score:

    AI-driven purchase timing recommendation.

  • Restock Forecasts:

    Enter ASIN to see restock estimates.

  • Emotion-Feedback Loop:

    AI-driven emotional insights on purchases.
  • (Coming Soon): URL Parameter Builder, API Scratchpad, Visual & Voice Interfaces, Niche & Lifestyle Filters, Social & Collaborative features.
