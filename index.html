Help & Usage Guide

  • Search Keywords: Enter terms and use Boolean operators (AND, OR, NOT).
  • Price Range ($): Minimum and maximum price (supports cents).
  • % Off Discount ≥: Filter by discount percentage (5% steps from 5% up to 100%).
  • Customer Review: Filter by star rating (1★ through 5★).
  • Sort By: Order results by relevance, price low→high, or price high→low.
  • Include Brands: Tag brands to include; check “Match only these brands” to exclude all others.
  • Exclude Brands: Tag brands to exclude.
  • Prime Only: Show only Prime-eligible products.
  • In Stock: Show only currently in-stock items.
  • Free Shipping: Filter for free-shipping items.
  • Sold & Shipped by Amazon: Only Amazon-sold items.
  • Lightning Deals: Active lightning deals only.
  • Coupons Available: Items with coupons.
  • Subscribe & Save: Subscribe & Save eligible items.
  • Amazon’s Choice: Amazon’s Choice products.
  • Best Seller: Amazon Best Sellers.
  • Free Returns: Items with free returns.
  • Gift Wrap Available: Items offering gift-wrap.
  • Enable Boolean Operators: Turn on/off Boolean parsing in keywords.
  • Field Filters: Advanced field queries (e.g. field-brand:Sony).
  • ASIN/ISBN Direct Lookup: Jump to a product by ASIN, ISBN or UPC.
  • Price Drop ≥ X% Below 90-Day Avg: Filter by drop vs. 90-day average price.
  • Coupon-Clippable Only: Show only coupon-clippable items.
  • Warehouse / Open-Box: Warehouse and open-box deals.
  • ▼ % vs. Avg (30/60/90-Day): Filter by percentage drop vs. short-term average.
  • ▲ Max 7-Day Volatility: Limit by 7-day price volatility.
  • Comparison & Research Aids: Side-by-side spec tables, verified-purchase reviews.
  • Personalization & AI-Driven: Buy-Now-or-Wait score, restock forecasts, emotion-feedback loop.
  • (Coming Soon): URL Parameter Builder, API Scratchpad, Visual & Voice Interfaces, Niche & Lifestyle Filters, Social & Collaborative features.
